The summit

The experience

The Summit is the geographical center of the the Breckenridge Ski Area. It also has the widest variety of terrain, from beginner trails to steep bowl terrain. Lower Peak 8 consists of beginner trails, as well as many intermediate trails mixed with some advanced runs.

In 2007, The Summit hosted the first Winter RadAir It was one of the first action sports tours for winter sports. The Summit continues to host the event annually in the 2nd or 3rd weekend in December each year. Many of the top athletes in action sports from around the world continue to participate in the RadAir Tour.
